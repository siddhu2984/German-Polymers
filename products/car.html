Car port Rooftop Module Mounting Structures

Download DocDownload PDF

Car Port Rooftop Module Mounting Structures are dual functioning. They support solar panels while providing a shaded area for vehicles beneath it. It helps in maximum utilization of available limited space for car parking in any premise without compromising the main use. In such way, sustainable energy can be generated from the idle source.

With experience of years and professionalism, we can provide custom car port rooftop module mounting structures with racking that can withstand common outdoor hazards and has longer life span. Theses solar panel racks can support solar panels of varying sizes.

Application

These can be applied at car parking space to integrate a sustainable energy generation at any premise.

Product Advantages:

  • Easy installation and custom design enhance the applicability of these structures at every kind of site.
  • Structural components are fabricated and designed in a very precise manner to reduce the installation, operation and maintenance cost.
  • Our structure can withstand common outdoor hazards that include stormy winds, heavy rains, corrosion etc.
  • From geological conditions to mounting foundations, due to inclusion of inclusion of advanced technology and design of component, we can provide customization in design at every level.
  • Every component is precisely checked and tested for their performance in unfavorable circumstances. These are highly durable and long-lasting.
  • Optimized manufacturing and customization make our structures cost friendly. Thus, break-even can be attained in less time.
