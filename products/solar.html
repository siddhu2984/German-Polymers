Solar Pump Module Mounting Structures

Solar Pump Module Mounting Structures

Download DocDownload PDF

Agriculture is the backbone of every country and water is its vital need for Agriculture purpose.  German Solar is a leading provider of solar pump for Water supply for Agriculture purpose , solar is one of the most economical and immediate solution for electrification of water pumping. Also, to grid connected agricultural field, solar pumps reduced the overall dependence on grid electricity. Further, production cost can be decreased.

German Solar is a leading provider of Solar Pump Module Mounting Structures in India.Our structures are designed with high quality for more durability and higher performance. Our mounting structures are designed in such a way that water pumps will get electricity in both the conditions i.e. with or without grid connection.

Application

Installation of solar water pumps and tanks at all kind of agricultural sites.

Product Advantages:

  • Easy installation, high durability and cost-effectiveness are the key usp of our products.
  • Structural components can withstand stormy winds, heavy rains and other environmental conditions in open terrain.
  • Structures are designed in such a way that mounted solar panels can harness the maximum energy from the sun, by orienting or aligning the payload to the sunlight.
  • Apart from supporting the solar cells, we provide structures for focusing mirrors, reflectors and lenses that are used for concentrated solar units.
  • These products are cost effective, durable and ensure high performance.
