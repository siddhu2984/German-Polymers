Ground Module Mounting Structures

Conceived out of the knowledge of solar industry and real world condition under which ground mounting solar installations have to perform, German Solar is a leading Design Manufacturing and Supply of peerless ground mounting solar structures. Every ground-mounting solar products are known for their high durability, adaptability and reliability. Considering the site variations and associated parameters, we provide appropriate ground mounting products and solutions only after quantitative and qualitative analysis of the site. The site will be assessed carefully for the availability of resources and feasibility of the installation for enhancing the output at optimized cost.

With the mission of transforming the energy sector and establishing new renewable energy milestones, German Solar has proven itself as a leading technology innovator, Design Manufacturing and Supply of solar solutions.

Application

Our ground mounting structures have huge application in installing photovoltaic at outdoors for utilizing the available ground space for solar energy production.

Product Advantages

  • With focus on user-experience, our precisely designed ground mounting structures are easy to install at all type of sites. Base plate and upper channels for installing the panels can be easily fixed by nuts & bolts. Also, presence of slots makes the assembly an easy task.
  • End-Clips and Mid-Clips enables easy fixation of upper channel and brackets thus avoids the practical issue of hole matching.
  • Major structural components viz. Column post, Vertical & Horizontal Rafters, Bracing, Purlins, Splice Plates and Brackets are tailored and designed in a optimized way to reduce the workmanship, site errors, transportation cost and difficulty in installation. Thus, makes the overall installation process easy and simple.
  • The incredible toughness, anti corrosion property and the structural design of these products allow their installation in almost every kind of climate and topography.
  • Raw material for every product is finely selected to ensure the long-lasting durability.
  • Low maintenance and optimized manufacturing make our product cost-friendly.
