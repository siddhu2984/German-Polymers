Jerlac High Temperature Coating ( Air Drying )

Product Description 

We are the manufacturer of Polysiloxane High Heat Resistant Air Drying Coating.

Need of the product

Due to high heat, Chimneys, Furnaces, Ovens, Boilers, Burning Stoves, Automobile Mufflers, Industrial Machinery, Plants and Pipes and Other heat producing units gets corroded very fast. It also causes risk of life of the technician and operator who is handling it and also reduces the efficiency of the industrial unit.

These exposed materials at higher temperature need thermal barrier for preventing excess heating up and causing risk.

Introducing:

Star Thermal Shield is a Polysiloxane based high heat resistant coating which can resist a temperature up to 600 degrees. Star Thermal Shield has high durability and self drying property paint. There is no need of baking for curing as it can dry with atmospheric air only. It has low reflectance and has excellent adhesion with GI Sheets, Iron Sheets, Stainless Steels and Casting Iron and Aluminum Substrates. Star Thermal Shield is available in both black and silver colors and can be used as a single coat on metal surfaces.

How to use

Thermal Shield forms thin hard coating of Polysiloxane polymer resin on the surface to be treated and provide protection against excess heating. It provides a thermal barrier to the metallic or steel surface.

Unique prepositions

  • One of the few paints in the world to have the Polysiloxane in it.
  • Heat resistant paint upto 600 degrees.
  • Room temperature cure for air drying.
  • Single coat requirement.
  • Easy application by brush or spray.
  • Highly durable.
  • No need of baking for curing

Where to use

The product can be applied on many appliances like Chimneys, Ovens, Barbeques, Mufflers and Other areas where temperatures are high. It can also be suitable for Stacks, Kilns, Breechings, Burning Stoves, Heat Exchangers, Boilers, Turbine Housing and Most essential piping in industrial plants.
