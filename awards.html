Awards

Waaree has acquired honors on numerous occasions from Industry, Government and Our Overseas Parnters.

Waaree Energies rated top performer in PVEL’s 2023 PV Module Reliability Scorecard

Waaree Energies Ltd, India’s largest solar panel manufacturer, has been recognized as a Top Performer in the 9th edition of PVEL’s prestigious annual PV Module Reliability Scorecard. It won the rating after its products met international quality and performance benchmarks set by PVEL.

Waaree Energies won the RE Brand of the Year award at the RenewX Awards 2023.

Yet another award! Waaree Energies won the RE Brand of the Year award at the RenewX Awards 2023. The award recognizes Waaree's exceptional contribution to the Renewal Energy industry in India.

Waaree Group has been listed as the leading module suppliers in the solar segment in India.

Once again Waaree stands out with the highest market share in India. In the 2023 quarter report of JMK Research, Waaree Group has been listed as the leading module suppliers in the solar segment in India. contributing to a 12.8% share of total shipments.

Best exporter of FY 2021-22

Waaree was recently recognized as the Best exporter for FY 2021-22. The award ceremony was organised by the office of the development commissioner, and evaluated by The SURSEZ unit holders Association.

Awarded Best brand by economic times

Waaree Energies has recently been conferred with 'The Economics times Best Brand award'. At core our belief lies in providing excellent product quality and associated services to all of our customers, while believing in Atmanirbhar Bharat, we are obliged to make our country self-sufficient in Solar Energy.

Visionary CleanTech Influencer of The Year 2021

World CleanTech Awards 2021 (WCA) powered by CleanTech Business Club recently honoured Dr. Hitesh Doshi with World CleanTech Award for Visionary Individuals: Visionary CleanTech Influencer of The Year
