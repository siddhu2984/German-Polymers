About Us

The Jerlac brand high performance coating is backed by years of experience. It’s brand well known for its pioneering spirit and commitment to quality. Always innovator, Jerlac brand high performance coatings has proven its leadership in the coating industry by consistently producing ideas and products that enhance performance, reduce cost and minimize application problems which exemplifies the competitive sustainable advantage over two decades which helped the company to associate with our major customers for a longer period of time.

Years Of Excellence in Service

German Polymers and Coatings Private Limited (GPC), is located @ S.No.123, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR) Kandanchavadi, Chennai – 600 096, Tamil Nadu, India. From 1994 till 1999 the unit was running under the name of German Paints and Chemicals and later reconstituted in to a Private Limited Company namely GERMAN POLYMERS AND COATINGS PVT LTD. The Plant employs well trained workforce to deliver qualitative products. The Company is having a State of the Art Manufacturing plant for Production of High Performance Protective Coatings. The Company caters their products under the brand name of “Jerlac”. All Varieties of Paints, Primers, Specialty Varnishes and Thinners are made for Industrial/Automotive Applications for both Domestic and International customers. The Plant is certified for ISO 9001-2015 and having a dedicated In-house laboratory with full-fledged equipment and apparatus for Development of Paints and Allied products suiting to the Customer’s specification. The In-house Laboratory is accredited with NABL Certification and ISO/IEC 17025:2005
